So, what is UAS technology used for?

Check out the links below to find out!

 


  

COMMERCIAL AERIAL SURVEILLANCE

Commercial aerial surveillance

can involve surveying of crops,

inspecting power lines and pipe lines,

livestock monitoring, and wild fire monitoring.

 

FILM MAKING

UAS technology is also being used in the filmmaking industry. They are being put to use in a variety of genres- everything from car commercials, to news programs, to televised sports- and are heralded for their ease, agility, and low cost.
 

SEARCH AND RESCUE

UAS technology has played a significant role in search and rescue operations. Thanks to features such as real-time camera footage and infrared thermal imaging technology, drones are amazingly helpful in these missions.
